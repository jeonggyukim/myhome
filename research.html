Jeong-Gyu Kim's Home Page

My main research interest is in understanding dynamics of H II regions and their impact on the surrounding interstellar medium. Using linear stability analysis as well as numerical simu- lations, I have previously studied gravitational instability of a thin layer confined by an external pressure (Kim et al. 2012), interfacial instability of evaporation fronts in the ISM (Kim & Kim 2013), and wiggle instability of galactic spiral shocks (Kim et al. 2014). I am currently writing a paper on the instability of ionization fronts surrounding H II regions.
